About EDEVELOPER

EDEVELOPER is a platform designed to help beginners learn and grow in the field of development. Whether you're interested in web development, Android development, or iOS development, EDEVELOPER provides resources, tutorials, and tools to guide you on your journey. Our mission is to make development accessible and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of their background or experience level.

The platform was created with a passion for coding and a desire to share knowledge. It started as a personal journey of learning and applying concepts, which eventually grew into a resourceful platform for others. Our History

The idea of EDEVELOPER was born during the first semester of the first year of college by Bhuvam Gautam. Initially, it began as a personal endeavor to learn coding and understand the fundamentals of development. The journey started with learning the basics, followed by applying the knowledge to real-world projects.

In 2024, Bhuvam Gautam decided to turn this passion into a platform that could help others. He dedicated himself to creating a space where beginners could find structured resources, interactive tools, and a supportive community. His love for coding and problem-solving, combined with a keen interest in technology, drove the development of EDEVELOPER.

Today, EDEVELOPER stands as a testament to the belief that anyone can learn and excel in development with the right guidance and resources. It continues to inspire and empower developers worldwide.
